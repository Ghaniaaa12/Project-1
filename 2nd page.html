Biryani

Biryani is he mixed rice dish orginating among the muslims of the India subcontinent. It is made with Indian spices,rice,and either meat,eggs or vegetables such as potatos. Biryani is one of the most popular dish in South asia, as well as among from the region.

Qorma

Korma or Qorma is a dish orginating in the Indian subcontinent, consisting of meat or vegetables braised with yogurt, water or stock, and spices to produce a thick sauce or gravy.

Kabab

Kabab is cooked meat dish, with its origins in middle eastern cuisines. Many variants are popular around the world

Fried Chicken

Fried chicken, also known as southern fried chicken, is a dish consisting of chicken piece that have been coaed with seasoned flour or batter and pan-fried, deep fried, pressure fried, or air fried. The breading adds a crisp coating or crust to the exterior of the chicken while reataining juice in the meat.

Gajar ka halwa

Gajar ka halwa, also known as gajorer halua, gajrela, gajar pak, and carrot halwa is a carrot-based sweet dessert pudding from the indian subcontinent. it is made by placing greated carrots in a pot containing a specific amount of water , milk and sugar , cardamon and then cooking while stirring regularly.
